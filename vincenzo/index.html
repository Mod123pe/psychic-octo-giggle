Song Joong Ki

Song Joong-ki is a South Korean actor, singer, and model. He was born on September 19, 1985 in Daejeon, South Korea. He is known for his roles in the TV shows Descendants of the Sun (2016), Sungkyunkwan Scandal (2010), and Vincenzo, and the film Space Sweepers (2021).
Song Joong-ki made his acting debut in the 2008 period film A Frozen Flower. He then appeared in several TV shows and films, eventually becoming a teen star and a star in both TV and film.
